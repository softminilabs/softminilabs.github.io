Terminology

Application Data Cache: A mechanism for storing data on a device. It can improve performance and allow certain web applications to work without an internet connection.

Personal Information: Information provided by a user that can be used to identify that user, such as name, phone number, address, or other related data.

IP Address: A unique number assigned to each device connected to the internet. IP addresses can often be used to identify the country or region where the device is located.

I. How We Collect and Use Your Information

1. Information you provide

  • Relevant personal information you provide when registering an account or using our services, such as your name, phone number, and similar details.
  • Information you share with others through our services, including information that you upload, submit, store, send, or receive.

2. Information obtained while you use our services

We collect information about the services you use and how you use them, such as when you access our services or view our content.

  • Device information: Hardware model, operating system details, mobile device version, device identifier, and screen-related information.
  • Log information: Software configuration, browser information, IP address, app version, and device identifiers.
  • Viewed or searched content: Search terms, URLs you visit, and related content details that you request through our services.
  • App and communication details: Information about apps or software you use with our services and communication time, data, and duration.
  • Shared content metadata: Date, time, or location attached to photos, videos, or other content shared through our services.
  • Photos, videos, and software information: With your authorization, our servers may extract related file and software details from your device in encrypted form to match display features.
  • Location information: You may authorize location services for location-based features, and you can stop collection by disabling location access on your device.

<h2>Overview</h2>

ZenPomo is a focus timer built for study, reading, writing, and deep work.  
It does more than count down time. It helps you block distractions, settle into a better rhythm, and build a steadier focus habit with clear stats, trends, and heatmaps.

Whether you are studying, getting through daily work, reading, or trying to use your attention more intentionally, ZenPomo is designed to make focus feel calmer, clearer, and easier to sustain.

### Why ZenPomo
- Start a focus session quickly without getting lost in settings
- Reduce distractions with app blocking and Strict Mode
- Use ambient sound and timer cues to create a more immersive focus experience
- Track your progress with focus stats, trends, heatmaps, and category insights
- Keep your focus visible with Home Screen widgets, Lock Screen widgets, and Live Activities

### Start focusing with less friction
- Start focus and break sessions in one tap
- Customize focus and break durations
- Automatically move to the next timer stage
- Use tags to organize different kinds of focus sessions
- Build a better rhythm with sound, structure, and pacing

### Reduce distractions and protect your time
- Block distracting apps during focus sessions
- Use strict mode to reduce interruptions
- Keep the screen awake while you focus
- Use reminders and Live Activities to stay on track

### See your progress clearly
- Review your focus data by day, week, month, and year
- Find your most productive hours with 24-hour distribution
- Use heatmaps to review your focus rhythm and consistency
- Use category breakdowns to understand where your time goes
- Turn every completed session into long-term momentum

### Bring focus to your system surfaces
- Home Screen widgets
- Lock Screen widgets
- Countdowns, today stats, and trends at a glance

### Zen Premium unlocks more
- Premium themes and deeper personalization
- More ambient sounds and immersive experiences
- iCloud sync
- More advanced features and future updates

If you want a focus app that feels calm, clear, and genuinely helps you reduce distractions and stay consistent, ZenPomo is built for you.
